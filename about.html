Who's Claire?

Born in China in 1996 and moved to Los Angeles at the age of eleven, Claire aspires to explore her multicultural background by means of digital design and product management. She studied Fine Arts, Cognitive Neuroscience, and Computer Science at the University of Pennsylvania and is now working at Adobe as an Associate Consultant/Product Owner who specializes in web content management.
