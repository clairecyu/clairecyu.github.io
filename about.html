Who's Claire?

Born in China in 1996 and moved to Los Angeles at the age of eleven, Claire aspires to explore her multicultural background by means of digital illustration and design. She is currently an undergraduate student studying Fine Arts, Cognitive Neuroscience, and Computer Science at the University of Pennsylvania. In the upcoming semester, Claire will be taking 3D Computer Modeling, Photography Practices, Fine Arts Senior Seminar, and various computer science courses. She is looking forward to incorporate design into her newfound interest in Computer Science.
